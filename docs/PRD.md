Product Requirements Document
Product Name

Merchant Trust & Fraud Detection Platform

Problem Statement

Marketplace ecosystems face increasing abuse from fraudulent merchants who exploit onboarding systems, create fake businesses, evade platform policies, and conduct harmful commercial activities.

Existing onboarding processes often create unnecessary friction for legitimate merchants while still allowing sophisticated abuse actors to bypass controls.

The challenge is protecting users and the platform without significantly impacting merchant growth.

Goals
Detect fraudulent merchants early
Improve onboarding efficiency
Reduce false positives
Support scalable trust & safety reviews
Improve merchant trust
Users
Legitimate Merchants

Need fast onboarding, transparent requirements, and predictable approval decisions.

Trust & Safety Analysts

Need tools to investigate suspicious merchants and enforce platform policies.

Platform Operations Teams

Need visibility into fraud trends, operational workload, and ecosystem health.

Key Features
Merchant risk scoring
Merchant verification workflow
Manual review queue
Fraud analytics dashboard
Trust & safety metrics
Success Metrics
Fraud detection recall
False positive rate
Merchant onboarding completion rate
Time-to-detection
Review SLA compliance
Risks & Tradeoffs

Increasing fraud detection sensitivity may increase false positives and negatively impact legitimate merchants.

The platform must balance ecosystem safety with business growth objectives.
